project

ROOTS

vision

To transform Nagalapuram into a beacon of learning that strengthens its position as a cultural nucleus while providing modern educational resources to tens of thousands of residents across dozens of villages in the Thoothukudi District.

mission

To establish and maintain a state-of-the-art library that honors local cultural heritage while providing comprehensive educational resources and innovative programming to Nagalapuram and its surrounding communities.
